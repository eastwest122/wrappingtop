What Is a Vehicle Wrapping Service?

A Vehicle Wrapping Service involves applying large sheets of premium-quality vinyl film to the surface of your vehicle. The wrap can cover the entire car (full wrap) or selected areas (partial wrap) depending on your desired look and budget.
